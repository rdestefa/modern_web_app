Champions List

Health

The amount of health points your champion has determines how much damage you can receive before dying. The greater your health, the more hits you can take.

Health Regen

For each point of health regeneration, your champion will passively restore that much heaevery 5 seconds.

Armor

Armor enables your champion to take reduced damage from physical attacks. The higher your armor, the less physical damage you will take.

Magic Resist

Magic resistance enables your champion to take reduced damage from special attacks such as spells. The higher your magic resistance, the less magical damage damage you will take.

Attack Damage

Your champion's attack damage determines how much damage each physical attack will do to your opponent. Some spells that do physical damage also scale off of your attack damage.

Attack Speed

The higher your attack speed, the faster your champion will attack his or her opponents. Attack speed reaches a cap at 2.5 attacks per second.

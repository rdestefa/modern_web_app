Abilities

Fury of the Xer'Sai (Passive)

Rek'Sai gains 25 fury each time she deals damage to an enemy. She loses 20 fury per second if she hasn't gained any in the last 5 seconds. When Rek'Sai burrows into the ground, she consumes her fury to heal herself over 3 seconds, or until she reaches full health.

Queen's Wrath / Prey Seeker (Q)

Unburrowed: Rek'Sai's next 3 basic attacks deal bonus physical damage in a small area of effect centered on her.

Burrowed: Rek'Sai fires a projectile in a target direction that deals physical damage to the first enemy hit, revealing them for 2.5 seconds.

Burrow / Unburrow (W)

Unburrowed: Rek'Sai burrows into the ground, reducing her sight radius but granting her bonus movement speed and access to an alternate set of abilities. This also activates Tremor Sense, which grants obscured vision of nearby enemy footsteps even when they cannot be seen.

Burrowed: Rek'Sai bursts out of the ground, dealing physical damage to and knocking up units above her.

Furious Bite / Tunnel (E)

Unburrowed: Rek'Sai bites an enemy, dealing physical damage. If Rek'Sai's fury bar is full, the bite deals double damage and the damage is converted to true damage.

Burrowed: Rek'Sai dashes in a target direction, leaving behind a tunnel that she can burrow through that lasts up to 10 minutes, or until an enemy destroys it. This tunnel can traverse uncrossable terrain, and Rek'Sai can have up to 8 tunnels on the map at any given time.

Void Rush (R)

Passive: When Rek'Sai damages an enemy, she applies Mark as Prey to them for 5 seconds.

Active: Rek'Sai selects an enemy with Mark as Prey and dashes to them, becomming unstoppable and vanishing for 1.5 seconds before leaping out of the ground and dealing physical damage to them based on their missing health.

Abilities

Perseverance (Passive)

Garen innately restores a percentage of his maximum health after not taking damage for 8 seconds.

Decisive Strike (Q)

Garen removes all slowing effects and gains bonus movement speed for a brief period. Additionally, his next basic attack within 4.5 seconds does bonus physical damage and silences the target for 1.5 seconds.

Courage (W)

Passive: Garen permanently gains a small amount of bonus armor and magic resistance every time he kills and enemy, up to a maximum of 30.

Active: Garen reduces incoming damage by 30% and gains a shield for a few seconds.

Judgement (E)

Garen rapidly spins his sword around his body, dealing physical damage for each spin to enemies within its range.

Demacian Justice (R)

Garen deals a large about of true damage to his target, based on their missing health.

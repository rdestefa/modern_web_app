Abilities

Hemorrhage (Passive)

When Darius deals damage to enemies, he causes them to bleed for periodic physical damage. This effect stacks up to 5 times. When 5 stacks have been applied to an enemy, or when Darius kills an enemy with Noxian Guillotine, he gains Noxian Might for 5 seconds.

Noxian Might: Darius gains a massive amount of bonus attack damage and instalty applies 5 stacks of Hemorrhage to enemies with his attacks.

Decimate (Q)

After a 0.75 second delay, Darius swings his axe around himself, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies. This damage is reduced for enemies hit by the handle of the axe. Darius heals for a percentage of his missing health per enemy hit by the blade.

Crippling Strike (W)

Darius' next basic attack deals bonus physical damage and greatly slows his target for 1 second. If Crippling Strike kills its target, it refunds its mana cost and half of its cooldown.

Apprehend (E)

Passive: Darius gains armor penetration, allowing his physical attacks to ignore a percentage of his target's armor.

Active: Darius pulls in all enemies in front of him with his axe.

Noxian Guillotine (R)

Darius leaps toward an enemy in an attempt to execute them, dealing true damage that increases with each stack of Hemorrhage on the target. If the target is killed, Darius instantly gains Noxian Might and the ability's cooldown is refunded for 20 seconds. At level 16, the cooldown is refunded completely.
